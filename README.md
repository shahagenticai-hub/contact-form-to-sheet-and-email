# Contact Form to Sheet & Email

An automated workflow built with **n8n** that captures customer inquiries through a contact form, saves them to **Google Sheets** and instantly sends a confirmation email to the customer.

## How It Works
1. Customer fills out a contact form with Name, Email, Description and Course Type
2. Form submission is automatically saved as a new row in **Google Sheets**
3. A confirmation email is instantly sent to the customer via **Gmail** thanking them for reaching out

## Tools Used
- n8n
- Google Sheets
- Gmail

## Real Life Use Cases
- 🎓 Online course platforms collecting student inquiries
- 💼 Freelancers and agencies capturing client leads
- 🛒 E-commerce businesses handling customer support queries
- 🏫 Training institutes managing course enrollment queries
